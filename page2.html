What is Project Management?

Project management in the IT sector is a multifaceted discipline that requires a combination of technical knowledge, leadership skills, and the ability to adapt to rapidly changing environments. Training programs play a pivotal role in equipping IT professionals with the tools and techniques needed to excel in project management roles. As organizations continue to rely on IT for their strategic initiatives, the investment in project management training becomes not only a necessity but a strategic imperative for success in the digital age.
